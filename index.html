WHO WE ARE

Blackoil Offshore Refined Oil Product Trading is a direct buyer and reseller of petroleum products and bi-products. We are located in the UAE one of the major world supplier countries in the world with high quality products of petroleum. We strive to deliver the highest quality petroleum products to our customers throughout the World. Our outstanding management team relationship with the ANDOC, ARAMCO Saudi and other major producers has earned us a priority treatment.

Our management and fulfillment team has the knowledge and dedication to fulfill contracts with efficient satisfactory manner. Through our contacts and business partners we can fulfill our customer's needs along wide range of petroleum products and bi-products.

Best Price

Best available prices is our promise to our customers. Our long term contracts are designed to ensure most competitive prices with the highest quality and reliability.

Our direct business relations and partnerships allow us the flexibility required to fulfill contracts without extra costs by cutting the middleman.

Our marketing and commercial team works hard to give our customers a peace of mind and save unrequired waste of efforts and time.
